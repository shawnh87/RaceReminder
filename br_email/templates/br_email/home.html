Welcome to RaceReminder

Who

Ever miss a race because you forgot to check BikeReg? Or maybe a race popped up after you last had a look? Do you think regular notifications about races would make your life just a little better?

If the answer is YES to any of the above, RaceReminder is for you.

What

RaceReminder sends you email notifications about upcoming races. That's it.

How

Simply register an account and fill out our distribution form. If you're familiar with BikeReg, the form questions will make sense. The key is to let us know the day and how frequently you'd like to be notified about upcoming races. Then you'll be race reminded.

Tired of your current settings? That's fine. Update any time.

Why

Why not? We love to race. You love to race. We all love when more people race. Let's keep the sport thriving.
